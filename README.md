This course introduces the theory, algorithms, and system implementations of **distributed optimization** for modern **machine learning and AI** workloads.  

Topics include:
- Consensus-based algorithms and federated learning  
- Parallel, asynchronous, and communication-efficient optimization  
- Resource-aware and robust distributed designs  
- Optimization foundations for large-scale foundation models  

> **Goal:** Equip students with both theoretical understanding and practical skills for scalable and trustworthy optimization in AI systems.
